About

I was eight years old the first time I understood what light actually does.

My father had taken me to the theatre. The final scene: a living room on stage, an artificial fire, warm sconces, candles. And I sat completely still, convinced I was in that room. Then the main lights came on, and in an instant, the room was gone.

The magic was never in the furniture. It was entirely, completely, in the light.

I founded Luminae after more than a decade designing and commissioning KNX automation, intelligent lighting, AV, and surveillance systems for premium residential and commercial clients. I bring one thing that is rare in this field: I think like a lighting designer and an engineer simultaneously.

Which means I can be at the table from the beginning — not called when the walls are already closed.
